# Our Teams

Kyndof is organized into focused teams, each with clear responsibilities.

## Teams

### [CEO Office](./ceo-office/)

### [Corporate Operations](./corp-ops/)

## How Teams Work Together

Teams collaborate using our [RABSIC framework](../how-we-work/making-decisions/rabsic/) to clarify who's responsible, accountable, and consulted on each decision.
