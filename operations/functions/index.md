# Functions

Organizational functions represent specialized capabilities and responsibilities within Kyndof. Each function owns specific processes, maintains standards, and contributes to value creation.

## Core Functions

### Creative & Design
- **[Fashion Design](/operations/functions/fashion-design/)** - Design development, pattern making, material research
- **[PR](/operations/functions/pr/)** - Public relations, brand communications, media outreach

### Sales & Marketing
- **[Sales](/operations/functions/sales/)** - Client acquisition, relationship management, order processing
- **[Marketing](/operations/functions/marketing/)** - Brand positioning, content creation, digital campaigns
- **[MD (Merchandising)](/operations/functions/md/)** - Product assortment, pricing strategy, inventory planning

### Operations & Supply Chain
- **[Operations](/operations/functions/operations/)** - Production coordination, quality control, workflow optimization
- **[Supply Chain Management](/operations/functions/supply-chain/)** - Vendor management, procurement, materials sourcing
- **[Logistics](/operations/functions/logistics/)** - Shipping, warehousing, distribution, delivery coordination

### Customer & Support
- **[CS (Customer Service)](/operations/functions/cs/)** - Customer support, issue resolution, satisfaction tracking

### Corporate Functions
- **[HR](/operations/functions/hr/)** - Recruitment, employee development, performance management
- **[Finance](/operations/functions/finance/)** - Financial planning, accounting, budgeting, reporting
- **[Legal](/operations/functions/legal/)** - Contracts, compliance, intellectual property, risk management
- **[IT](/operations/functions/it/)** - Systems management, infrastructure, technical support
- **[GA (General Administration)](/operations/functions/ga/)** - Facilities, office management, administrative support

### Strategic
- **[Strategy](/operations/functions/strategy/)** - Strategic planning, market analysis, business development
- **[IR (Investor Relations)](/operations/functions/ir/)** - Investor communications, fundraising, stakeholder engagement

## Function Structure

Each function contains:
- **Overview** - Mission, scope, key responsibilities
- **Processes** - Documented workflows with steps and SOPs
- **RABSIC Matrix** - Accountability assignments for key decisions
- **Tools & Systems** - Technology stack and platforms used

## Cross-Functional Collaboration

Many processes require coordination across functions:
- **Product Launch**: Fashion Design → Marketing → Sales → Logistics
- **Custom Orders**: Sales → Fashion Design → Operations → Quality Control
- **Strategic Planning**: Strategy → Finance → All Functions

See [Value Streams](/operations/value-streams/) for end-to-end workflows.
