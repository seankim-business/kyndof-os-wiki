# Marketing Function

## Mission
Build brand awareness, generate qualified leads, and position Kyndof as the premier custom costume and fashion design partner for theater, film, and events.

## Scope
- Brand strategy and positioning
- Content creation and distribution
- Digital marketing (website, SEO, social media)
- Lead generation and nurturing
- Event marketing and partnerships
- Portfolio and case study development
- Public relations and media outreach

## Key Responsibilities

### Brand Management
- Define and maintain brand voice, visual identity
- Ensure brand consistency across all touchpoints
- Develop messaging frameworks for key audiences
- Manage brand assets and guidelines

### Content Strategy
- Create editorial calendar aligned with business goals
- Produce blog posts, case studies, portfolio pieces
- Design visual content (images, videos, graphics)
- Distribute content across channels (website, social, email)

### Lead Generation
- Drive website traffic through SEO and content
- Manage social media presence (Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest)
- Run targeted ad campaigns (Google, Meta)
- Nurture leads through email marketing

### Partnership & Events
- Identify partnership opportunities (theaters, production companies)
- Attend industry events and tradeshows
- Coordinate sponsorships and collaborations
- Build relationships with influencers and press

## Core Processes
- **[Content Creation Workflow](/operations/functions/marketing/processes/content-creation/)** - Ideation through publication for all content types

## Tools & Systems
- **Notion**: Content calendar, campaign tracking, asset library
- **Canva**: Graphic design and visual content creation
- **Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest**: Social media platforms
- **Google Analytics**: Website traffic and conversion tracking
- **Mailchimp**: Email marketing and automation
- **Ahrefs**: SEO and competitive analysis

## Key Metrics
- Website traffic (monthly unique visitors)
- Lead generation (MQLs per month, target: 20)
- Social media engagement (followers, engagement rate)
- Content performance (views, shares, time on page)
- Email performance (open rate >25%, click rate >3%)
- Cost per lead (CPL)
- Marketing-sourced revenue

## Team Structure
- **Marketing Director** (A) - Strategy, budget, team leadership
- **Content Creator** (R) - Writing, design, production
- **Social Media Manager** (R) - Platform management, community engagement
- **Marketing Coordinator** (S) - Administrative support, analytics reporting
