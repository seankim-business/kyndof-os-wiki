# Sales Function

## Mission
Drive revenue growth through exceptional client relationships, consultative selling, and seamless order fulfillment for custom costume and fashion production.

## Scope
- B2B sales to production companies, theaters, costume designers
- B2C sales for high-end custom pieces
- Client relationship management throughout lifecycle
- Order intake, tracking, and coordination with production
- Pricing and contract negotiation

## Key Responsibilities

### Client Acquisition
- Identify and qualify leads from theater, film, TV, events industries
- Conduct needs assessments and consultative selling
- Present portfolio and capabilities to potential clients
- Manage proposal and contract processes

### Relationship Management
- Serve as primary client contact throughout engagement
- Understand evolving client needs and production schedules
- Proactive communication on order status and timelines
- Upsell and cross-sell opportunities

### Order Management
- Intake and documentation of custom specifications
- Coordinate with Fashion Design for feasibility and timelines
- Pricing in collaboration with Finance and Operations
- Order tracking from contract to delivery

## Core Processes
- **[Client Onboarding](/operations/functions/sales/processes/client-onboarding/)** (0.5 hours) - Intake and setup for new clients
- **[Order Management](/operations/functions/sales/processes/order-management/)** - End-to-end order lifecycle

## Tools & Systems
- **Notion**: Client database, order tracking, pipeline management
- **Slack**: #sales channel for coordination
- **Google Drive**: Proposal templates, contract storage
- **QuickBooks**: Invoicing and payment tracking
