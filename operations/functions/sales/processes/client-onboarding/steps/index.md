# Client Onboarding Steps

Sequential workflow for onboarding new clients into Kyndof systems and processes.

## Step Sequence

### 1. [Initial Contact](/operations/functions/sales/processes/client-onboarding/steps/01-initial-contact/)
**Duration:** 10 minutes
**Owner:** Account Manager

First interaction with new client. Gather basic information, set expectations, schedule needs assessment call.

---

### 2. [Needs Assessment](/operations/functions/sales/processes/client-onboarding/steps/02-needs-assessment/)
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Owner:** Account Manager

Deep dive into client requirements, production schedules, budget constraints, and collaboration preferences.

---

### 3. [Proposal Development](/operations/functions/sales/processes/client-onboarding/steps/03-proposal/)
**Duration:** 10 minutes
**Owner:** Account Manager

Create customized proposal based on needs assessment. Define scope, timeline, pricing, and deliverables.

---

### 4. [Contract Execution](/operations/functions/sales/processes/client-onboarding/steps/04-contract/)
**Duration:** 5 minutes
**Owner:** Sales Director (approval), Account Manager (execution)

Finalize contract, obtain signatures, initiate first project, complete Notion profile.

---

## Total Process Time
**Target:** 30-40 minutes (excluding client decision time between steps)

## Handoff Points
- After Step 4 → Handoff to Operations for production planning
- After Step 4 → Handoff to Finance for invoicing setup
- After Step 4 → Notify Fashion Design of incoming project requirements
