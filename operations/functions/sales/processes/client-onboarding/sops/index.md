# Client Onboarding SOPs

Standard Operating Procedures that support the Client Onboarding process.

## Available SOPs

### [Client Communication SOP](/operations/functions/sales/processes/client-onboarding/sops/sop-client-communication/)
Standards and templates for professional client communication throughout onboarding.

**Covers:**
- Email templates (welcome, follow-up, proposal)
- Phone call scripts
- Communication tone and brand voice
- Response time expectations
- Escalation protocols

---

### [Proposal Template SOP](/operations/functions/sales/processes/client-onboarding/sops/sop-proposal-template/)
Standard structure and content for client proposals.

**Covers:**
- Proposal sections and required content
- Pricing presentation formats
- Visual timeline creation
- Terms and conditions
- Revision and approval process

---

## SOP Maintenance

SOPs are reviewed and updated quarterly to reflect:
- Process improvements and lessons learned
- Template refinements based on client feedback
- New tools or systems integration
- Legal or compliance requirement changes

## Suggesting Improvements

To suggest SOP improvements:
1. Post in #process-improvements Slack channel
2. Add entry to "SOP Feedback" Notion database
3. Contact Sales Director directly for urgent changes

All suggestions reviewed monthly at Sales team meeting.
