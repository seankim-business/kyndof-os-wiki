# Client Onboarding Process

## Overview
Systematic intake and setup for new clients to ensure complete information capture, proper system configuration, and exceptional first impression.

**Duration:** 0.5 hours
**Owner:** Sales (Account Manager)
**Tool:** Notion Client Database
**Trigger:** New client signs first contract

## Objectives
- Capture complete client profile and preferences
- Establish communication protocols and points of contact
- Set expectations for collaboration and timelines
- Create foundation for long-term relationship

## Success Criteria
- Client profile 100% complete in Notion
- Communication preferences documented
- First project scope agreed and documented
- Client satisfaction score ≥ 4/5 on onboarding survey

## Metrics Tracked
- Time to complete onboarding (target: ≤ 30 min)
- Profile completeness (target: 100%)
- Client satisfaction on onboarding (target: ≥ 4/5)
- Time from contract to first project kickoff (target: ≤ 2 days)
