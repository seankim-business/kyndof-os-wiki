# Step 1: Order Receipt

**Duration:** 1 hour
**Owner:** Account Manager
**Tool:** Notion, Slack, Google Drive

## Objective
Set up order in all systems, coordinate internal kickoff, and ensure seamless handoff to production.

## Inputs
- Signed contract
- Deposit payment confirmation (from Finance)
- Needs assessment notes and proposal

## Activities

### 1. Create Order Record in Notion (15 min)
**Order Database Fields:**
- Order ID: Auto-generated (format: `ORD-YYYY-MM-###`)
- Client Name: [Link to client record]
- Project Name: [Descriptive title]
- Order Status: "Active - Design Phase"
- Contract Value: $[Amount]
- Deposit Received: âœ“
- Delivery Date: [Target date]
- Account Manager: [Your name]

**Attach Documents:**
- Signed contract PDF
- Approved proposal
- Design references/mood boards
- Measurement sheets (if received)

### 2. Create Project Slack Channel (5 min)
- Channel name: `#project-[client-name-short]`
- Add members: Account Manager, Fashion Design lead, Operations coordinator, Finance (for invoicing)
- Pin key documents: Contract, proposal, timeline
- Post kickoff message with order summary

### 3. Schedule Internal Kickoff Meeting (10 min)
**Attendees:** Account Manager, Fashion Design lead, Operations coordinator
**Duration:** 30 minutes
**Agenda:**
- Review creative brief and client vision
- Confirm timeline and production capacity
- Identify potential risks or constraints
- Assign design lead and production coordinator
- Set first client touchpoint (design review date)

### 4. Brief Fashion Design Team (15 min)
Create handoff document in Notion:
- Client background and aesthetic preferences
- Detailed scope from proposal
- Reference materials and inspiration
- Timeline and milestone dates
- Special requirements (movement, durability, historical accuracy)
- Budget allocation for materials

### 5. Brief Operations Team (10 min)
Share production requirements:
- Number of pieces and complexity level
- Estimated production hours
- Material sourcing needs
- Fitting schedule requirements
- Delivery logistics (location, packaging)

### 6. Set Client Communication Cadence (5 min)
- Agree on update frequency (weekly recommended)
- Confirm preferred communication method
- Schedule first design review meeting
- Add to Account Manager's recurring task list

## Outputs
- Order record created in Notion (status: "Active - Design Phase")
- Project Slack channel active with all stakeholders
- Internal kickoff meeting completed or scheduled
- Design and Operations teams briefed and ready
- Client communication plan established

## Success Criteria
- All systems updated within 24 hours of contract signing
- Kickoff meeting held within 3 business days
- Design work begins within 5 business days
- Client receives kickoff email with timeline
