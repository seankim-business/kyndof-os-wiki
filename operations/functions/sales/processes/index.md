# Sales Processes

Documented workflows owned and maintained by the Sales function.

## Active Processes

### [Client Onboarding](/operations/functions/sales/processes/client-onboarding/)
**Duration:** 0.5 hours
**Frequency:** Per new client
**Tool:** Notion

Initial intake and setup for new clients entering the Kyndof ecosystem. Ensures complete information capture, proper system setup, and smooth handoff to fulfillment.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Client profile created in Notion
- Contact information and preferences documented
- Communication channels established
- First project scoped and initiated

---

### [Order Management](/operations/functions/sales/processes/order-management/)
**Duration:** Varies by order complexity
**Frequency:** Per order
**Tool:** Notion, Slack

End-to-end lifecycle management from order receipt through delivery coordination. Ensures accurate specifications, realistic timelines, and proactive client communication.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Order specifications fully documented
- Production timeline confirmed with Operations
- Client receives regular status updates
- Successful delivery and client satisfaction

---

## Process Improvement

Sales processes are reviewed quarterly for efficiency gains. Suggestions for improvements should be submitted via:
- Slack: #process-improvements channel
- Notion: Process Feedback database
- Direct to Sales Director
