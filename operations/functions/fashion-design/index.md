# Fashion Design Function

## Mission
Create exceptional custom costume and fashion designs that bring client visions to life while maintaining artistic excellence and technical precision.

## Scope
- Concept development and design sketches
- Historical research and period accuracy
- Material research and selection
- Pattern making and technical drawings
- Prototype development and fittings
- Design system and standards maintenance

## Key Responsibilities

### Design Development
- Translate client briefs into visual concepts
- Create multiple design options with sketches and renderings
- Research period, cultural, and thematic accuracy
- Present designs to clients for approval
- Incorporate feedback and refine designs

### Technical Execution
- Create accurate technical drawings and specifications
- Develop custom patterns from measurements
- Select appropriate materials, fabrics, and embellishments
- Coordinate with Operations on construction feasibility
- Oversee prototype development and fitting adjustments

### Quality & Standards
- Maintain design quality and artistic integrity
- Ensure historical accuracy for period pieces
- Define construction standards and techniques
- Build and maintain material and supplier library
- Document successful designs for future reference

## Core Processes
- **[Design Development](/operations/functions/fashion-design/processes/design-development/)** - From brief analysis through final approved design
- **[Pattern Making](/operations/functions/fashion-design/processes/pattern-making/)** - Measurement through fitted mockup

## Tools & Systems
- **Adobe Creative Suite**: Illustrator (sketches), Photoshop (rendering)
- **Notion**: Design project tracker, material library, client feedback
- **Pinterest**: Mood boards and inspiration
- **Google Drive**: Design files, technical drawings, pattern files

## Key Metrics
- Design approval rate (first presentation, target: >70%)
- Client design satisfaction (target: ≥4.5/5)
- Pattern fit accuracy (first fitting, target: >85% fit without major alterations)
- Time to first design presentation (from brief, target: ≤7 days)
- Historical accuracy rating (for period work, target: 100%)

## Team Structure
- **Lead Designer** (A) - Creative direction, client presentations, final approval
- **Assistant Designers** (R) - Sketch development, research, material sourcing
- **Pattern Maker** (R) - Technical pattern development, fitting coordination
- **Design Coordinator** (S) - Project tracking, file management, admin support
