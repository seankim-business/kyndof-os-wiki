# Order Reception Activities

The Order Reception stage consists of three core activities that must be completed within 1 business day.

## Activity Sequence

```
1. Receive Brief (30-60 min)
         ↓
2. Timeline Estimation (15-30 min)
         ↓
3. Client Communication (30-45 min)
```

## Activities Overview

### [Receive Brief](receive-brief.md)
**Duration:** 30-60 minutes
**Owner:** Client Relations Manager
**Output:** Completed brief questionnaire

Initial consultation to capture all client requirements.

### [Timeline Estimation](timeline-estimation.md)
**Duration:** 15-30 minutes
**Owner:** Client Relations Manager (with Production Manager input)
**Output:** Timeline estimate with best/typical/worst case scenarios

Calculate realistic delivery timeline based on complexity and current workload.

### [Client Communication](client-communication.md)
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes
**Owner:** Client Relations Manager
**Output:** Proposal presented, agreement signed

Present timeline and cost estimate, address questions, finalize agreement.

## Activity Dependencies

```
Receive Brief ────────────┐
                          ▼
                   Timeline Estimation
                          │
                          ▼
                   Client Communication
```

**Note:** Timeline estimation cannot begin until brief is complete. Client communication cannot begin until timeline is estimated.

## Time Budget

| Activity | Minimum | Typical | Maximum |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Receive Brief | 30 min | 45 min | 60 min |
| Timeline Estimation | 15 min | 20 min | 30 min |
| Client Communication | 30 min | 40 min | 45 min |
| **Total Stage** | **75 min** | **105 min** | **135 min** |

Most projects complete within 2 hours of initial contact.

## Quality Checklist

Before moving to the next stage, verify:

- [ ] All brief questions answered (no "TBD" or blank fields)
- [ ] Reference images collected and saved
- [ ] Client measurements provided or measurement session scheduled
- [ ] Timeline estimate approved by Production Manager
- [ ] Cost estimate approved by client
- [ ] Agreement signed by both parties
- [ ] Project folder created in Notion
- [ ] Creative Director notified
