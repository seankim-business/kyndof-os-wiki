# Activity: cutting

**Owner:** Production Team

## Purpose

Accurately cut all fabric pieces according to pattern

## Process

1. Pre-wash fabric if required
2. Press all fabric flat
3. Layout pattern pieces efficiently
4. Mark grain lines and notches
5. Cut with sharp scissors/rotary cutter
6. Transfer markings to fabric
7. Organize cut pieces by garment section
