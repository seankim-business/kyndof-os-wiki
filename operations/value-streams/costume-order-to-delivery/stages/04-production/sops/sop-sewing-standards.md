# SOP: sewing standards

**Purpose:** Maintain consistent construction quality

## Steps

1. **Seam Allowance** - Standard 5/8" unless otherwise marked
2. **Stitch Length** - 2.5mm for wovens, 3mm for knits
3. **Pressing** - Press seams after each step
4. **Backstitching** - Always backstitch at seam start/end
5. **Thread Tension** - Adjust for fabric type
6. **Finish Seams** - Serge, zigzag, or bind raw edges
7. **Quality Check** - Inspect each seam before proceeding
