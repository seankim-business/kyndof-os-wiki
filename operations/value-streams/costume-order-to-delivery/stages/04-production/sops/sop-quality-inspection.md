# SOP: quality inspection

**Purpose:** Systematic quality verification before delivery

## Steps

1. **Visual Inspection** - Check for loose threads, raw edges, stains
2. **Seam Test** - Gently tug all seams to verify security
3. **Closure Test** - Zip, button, hook all closures 3x times
4. **Fit Verification** - Check against measurements on form
5. **Design Match** - Compare to approved design sketches
6. **Embellishment Check** - Verify all beads/trim secure
7. **Documentation** - Complete quality checklist, photograph
8. **Pass/Fail Decision** - Sign off or document rework needed
