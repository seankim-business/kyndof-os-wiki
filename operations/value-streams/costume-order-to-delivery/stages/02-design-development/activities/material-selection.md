# Activity: Material Selection

**Duration:** 1-2 days
**Owner:** Creative Director (with Design Assistant support)
**Output:** Complete material specifications with suppliers, SKUs, and cost estimates

## Process

### 1. Fabric Selection
- Primary fabric (base garment)
- Lining fabric
- Interfacing/stabilizers
- Specialty fabrics (leather, vinyl, metallic, etc.)

### 2. Trims & Notions
- Buttons, zippers, hooks
- Ribbon, lace, binding
- Elastic, boning, grommets

### 3. Embellishments
- Beads, sequins, crystals
- Embroidery thread
- Appliqu√© materials

### 4. Special Elements
- Foam for armor
- Thermoplastics (Worbla)
- LED components
- Prosthetic materials
