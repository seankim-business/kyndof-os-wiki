# Design Development Activities

Three core activities transform the client brief into an approved, production-ready design.

## Activity Sequence

```
1. Concept Development (2-3 days)
         ↓
2. Material Selection (1-2 days)
         ↓
3. Design Approval (2 days)
```

## Activities Overview

### [Concept Development](concept-development.md)
**Duration:** 2-3 days
**Owner:** Creative Director
**Output:** 3-5 concept sketches with annotations

Research character/period and create multiple design directions.

### [Material Selection](material-selection.md)
**Duration:** 1-2 days
**Owner:** Creative Director (with Design Assistant support)
**Output:** Material specifications with swatches and suppliers

Identify fabrics, trims, closures for each concept.

### [Design Approval](design-approval.md)
**Duration:** 2 days
**Owner:** Creative Director
**Output:** Client-signed approval with selected concept

Present concepts, gather feedback, finalize design.

## Time Budget

| Activity | Minimum | Typical | Maximum |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Concept Development | 2 days | 2.5 days | 3 days |
| Material Selection | 1 day | 1.5 days | 2 days |
| Design Approval | 1.5 days | 2 days | 2.5 days |
| **Total Stage** | **4.5 days** | **6 days** | **7.5 days** |
