# SOP: Material Sourcing

**Purpose:** Find and cost materials reliably and efficiently

## Steps

1. **Identify Requirements** - List all fabrics, trims, notions needed
2. **Check Inventory** - See what we already have in stock
3. **Source New Materials** - Contact preferred suppliers for quotes
4. **Document Options** - Record 2-3 options per item with pricing
5. **Verify Availability** - Confirm stock and lead times
6. **Create Spec Sheet** - Complete material list with suppliers and SKUs

## Preferred Suppliers

- **Mood Fabrics** - Specialty fabrics, silks, wools
- **Joann Fabrics** - Cottons, notions, general supplies
- **TNT Cosplay Supply** - Foam, thermoplastics, armor materials
- **Amazon** - Trims, embellishments, closures
