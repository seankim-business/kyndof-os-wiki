# SOP: Delivery Checklist

**Purpose:** Ensure complete, professional delivery every time

## Pre-Delivery Checklist

- [ ] Final fitting scheduled and confirmed
- [ ] Costume passed quality inspection
- [ ] Care instructions printed
- [ ] Garment bag or box prepared
- [ ] Final invoice generated
- [ ] Portfolio photos taken

## During Delivery Checklist

- [ ] Client tries on costume
- [ ] Fit confirmed or minor adjustments made
- [ ] Care instructions reviewed with client
- [ ] Final payment processed
- [ ] Satisfaction survey link sent
- [ ] Support contact info provided

## Post-Delivery Checklist

- [ ] Project marked complete in Notion
- [ ] Final payment confirmed by Finance
- [ ] Portfolio photos added to website
- [ ] Client feedback documented
- [ ] Thank you email sent (24 hours later)
- [ ] Request referral or testimonial (1 week later)
