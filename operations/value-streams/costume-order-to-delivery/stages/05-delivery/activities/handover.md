# Activity: handover

**Owner:** Client Relations Manager

## Purpose

Deliver costume and ensure client satisfaction

## Process

1. **Meet Client** - At studio or deliver to location
2. **Present Costume** - Open packaging, show final product
3. **Review Care Instructions** - Highlight key points
4. **Collect Final Payment** - Process remaining 25%
5. **Satisfaction Survey** - Request completion within 7 days
6. **Offer Support** - Remind of 30-day support policy
7. **Thank Client** - Express gratitude, request referrals
