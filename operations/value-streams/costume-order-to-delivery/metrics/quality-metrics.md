# Metric: Quality Scores

Quality measurement across all value stream stages.

## Quality Metrics by Stage

### Order Reception

**Brief Completeness**
- **Definition:** Percentage of briefs with all required fields filled
- **Target:** >95%
- **Measurement:** At stage completion
- **Current:** 92% (Q4 2025)

**Timeline Accuracy**
- **Definition:** Actual delivery vs. estimated timeline (within ±2 days)
- **Target:** >80%
- **Measurement:** At project completion
- **Current:** 78% (Q4 2025)

### Design Development

**First-Round Approval Rate**
- **Definition:** Client approves one of initial concepts without requesting major changes
- **Target:** >60%
- **Measurement:** After client design review
- **Current:** 65% (Q4 2025)

**Revision Count**
- **Definition:** Average number of revision rounds per project
- **Target:** <2
- **Measurement:** At stage completion
- **Current:** 1.8 (Q4 2025)

**Material Accuracy**
- **Definition:** Materials as-specified are available when ordered
- **Target:** >95%
- **Measurement:** During production material collection
- **Current:** 88% (Q4 2025)

### Pattern Making

**First-Fit Success**
- **Definition:** Costume fits without major alterations after first mockup/fitting
- **Target:** >85%
- **Measurement:** After mockup or first fitting
- **Current:** 87% (Q4 2025)

**Pattern Accuracy**
- **Definition:** Measurements transfer correctly from brief to pattern
- **Target:** >95%
- **Measurement:** At quality inspection
- **Current:** 96% (Q4 2025)

### Production

**First-Pass Quality**
- **Definition:** Costume passes quality inspection without rework
- **Target:** >90%
- **Measurement:** At quality inspection
- **Current:** 91% (Q4 2025)

**On-Time Production**
- **Definition:** Production stage completes within estimated timeframe
- **Target:** >85%
- **Measurement:** At stage completion
- **Current:** 82% (Q4 2025)

### Delivery

**Fit Success**
- **Definition:** No major alterations needed at final fitting
- **Target:** >95%
- **Measurement:** At final fitting
- **Current:** 94% (Q4 2025)

**Client Satisfaction**
- **Definition:** Client satisfaction score >8/10
- **Target:** >90%
- **Measurement:** 7 days after delivery (satisfaction survey)
- **Current:** 93% (Q4 2025)

**On-Time Delivery**
- **Definition:** Costume delivered by promised date
- **Target:** >85%
- **Measurement:** At handover
- **Current:** 78% (Q4 2025)
