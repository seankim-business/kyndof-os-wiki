# SOPs By Process

Standard Operating Procedures organized by end-to-end workflows that span multiple departments.

## Purpose

Some business processes involve multiple teams working sequentially or in parallel. This view presents SOPs organized by complete workflows rather than departmental ownership.

## Available Process Flows

### [Order to Delivery](/wiki/sops/by-process/order-to-delivery/)
Complete customer fulfillment process from initial inquiry through final delivery.

**Departments Involved:** Brand Ops (Sales) → 2000atelier (Design, Pattern, Production)

**Key Milestones:**
1. Client inquiry and qualification
2. Quote and contract
3. Design brief and concept
4. Pattern development
5. Production (cutting, sewing, finishing)
6. Quality inspection
7. Client delivery

**Timeline:** Typically 4-8 weeks depending on complexity

---

### [Hiring](/wiki/sops/by-process/hiring/)
Recruitment and onboarding process from job posting through employee's first 90 days.

**Departments Involved:** Corp Ops (HR) → Hiring Manager → HR

**Key Milestones:**
1. Job requisition and approval
2. Job posting and candidate sourcing
3. Interview process
4. Offer letter and negotiation
5. Background check and paperwork
6. Onboarding and training
7. 90-day review

**Timeline:** Typically 4-6 weeks (posting to start date)

---

### [Content Creation](/wiki/sops/by-process/content-creation/)
Content development process from concept through publication and promotion.

**Departments Involved:** Brand Ops (Marketing) → 2000atelier (Design for photoshoots) → Marketing

**Key Milestones:**
1. Content planning and calendar
2. Creative brief development
3. Production (photoshoot, writing, design)
4. Review and approval
5. Publishing
6. Promotion and distribution
7. Performance tracking

**Timeline:** Varies by content type (1 day - 4 weeks)

## How to Use Process Views

**When to use By Process:**
- You need to understand the full end-to-end workflow
- You're training someone on a cross-functional process
- You're troubleshooting delays or handoff issues
- You're optimizing cycle time across departments

**When to use By Department:**
- You need a specific procedure within one team
- You're looking for all SOPs owned by a department
- You're conducting departmental training or audits

## Process Metrics

Each process view includes:
- **Cycle Time:** Total time from process start to completion
- **Handoff Points:** Where responsibility transfers between teams
- **Common Delays:** Typical bottlenecks and mitigation strategies
- **Quality Gates:** Critical checkpoints with pass/fail criteria
