## Process Overview

Sales drives revenue by qualifying leads, preparing accurate quotes, and converting prospects into active clients.

### Sales Workflow
```
Inquiry Received → [SOP-100] Qualification → [SOP-101] Quote Preparation →
[SOP-102] Order Confirmation → Design Handoff
```

## Key Roles

- **Sales Manager:** Owns sales process, approves pricing, manages team
- **Sales Representative:** Executes inquiry response, qualification, quoting
- **Senior Sales Rep:** Handles complex deals, backup for Sales Rep

## Quality Metrics

- **Response Time:** 100% within 4 hours
- **Lead Qualification Rate:** ≥60%
- **Quote Conversion Rate:** ≥40%
- **Average Quote Value:** $2,500+
