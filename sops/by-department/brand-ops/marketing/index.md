## Process Overview

Marketing drives brand awareness and client acquisition through strategic content and campaigns.

### Marketing Workflow
```
Content Planning → SOP-110: Content Creation → SOP-111: Social Posting →
SOP-112: Campaign Launch → Performance Tracking
```

## Key Roles

- **Marketing Manager:** Owns marketing strategy, brand guidelines
- **Marketing Coordinator:** Executes content creation, posting schedules
- **Content Creator:** Produces photography, video, written content

## Quality Metrics

- **Posting Consistency:** 100% (no missed scheduled posts)
- **Engagement Rate:** ≥3% average (likes, comments, shares / followers)
- **Lead Generation:** ≥10 qualified leads per month from marketing
