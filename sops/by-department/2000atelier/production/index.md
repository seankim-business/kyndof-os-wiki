## Process Overview

Production transforms approved designs and patterns into finished garments through systematic fabrication and quality control.

### Production Workflow
```
Pattern Received → [SOP-020] Cutting → [SOP-021] Sewing →
[SOP-022] Finishing → [SOP-023] Quality Inspection → Client Delivery
```

## Key Roles

- **Production Manager:** Owns production workflow, resource allocation, quality metrics
- **Cutter:** Executes fabric cutting per [SOP-020](./sop-020-cutting-standards/)
- **Sewing Team Lead:** Manages sewing team, quality standards enforcement
- **Finisher:** Executes finishing procedures (hems, trim, buttons, pressing)
- **QA Inspector:** Performs final quality inspection before delivery

## Quality Metrics

- **Cutting Accuracy:** ≥98% (pieces requiring no re-cutting)
- **Fabric Waste:** ≤15% average
- **Sewing Quality:** 100% (zero defects at final inspection)
- **On-Time Delivery:** ≥90%
