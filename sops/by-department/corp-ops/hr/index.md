## Process Overview

HR builds and supports the team through systematic talent acquisition and employee support.

### HR Workflow
```
Requisition → SOP-200: Job Posting → Candidate Sourcing →
SOP-201: Interview Process → SOP-202: Offer Letter →
SOP-203: Onboarding → 90-Day Review
```

## Key Roles

- **HR Manager:** Owns HR processes, compliance, employee relations
- **Hiring Manager:** Conducts interviews, makes hiring decisions
- **HR Coordinator:** Executes posting, scheduling, paperwork

## Quality Metrics

- **Time to Fill:** ≤30 days (requisition to offer acceptance)
- **Offer Acceptance Rate:** ≥80%
- **New Hire Retention (90 days):** ≥90%
- **Onboarding Satisfaction:** ≥4.0/5.0
